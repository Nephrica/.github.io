About Nephrica

Nephrica is focused on fostering collaboration and awareness in nephrology, with an emphasis on supporting communities across Africa through education, partnership, and outreach.

Education

Sharing nephrology knowledge and resources to strengthen medical learning and professional development.

Collaboration

Building partnerships between students, physicians, and organizations to support kidney health initiatives.

Impact

Promoting meaningful outreach and sustainable support for nephrology care in underserved regions.
